How to breathe

New here? Just breathe along with the circle.

Breathe in

slowly, through your nose

  1. 1 Breathe in
  2. 2 Hold
  3. 3 Breathe out
  4. 4 Hold

1. Breathe in slowly through your nose — fill up, like the circle.

2. Hold gently for a few seconds.

3. Breathe out slowly through your mouth — let the circle empty.

4. Hold gently again before the next breath.

That's one breath. The app guides the timing for you.
